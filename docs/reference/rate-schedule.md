## Billing Cycle

### Definition

A **billing week** runs from:
- **Start:** Friday 12:00 AM UTC
- **End:** Thursday 11:59 PM UTC

### What Counts

Tasks count toward a billing week when they are **accepted**, not when submitted.

| Event | Counts For |
|-------|------------|
| Task accepted Monday | That week's billing |
| Task submitted Monday, accepted next Tuesday | Next week's billing |

### Payment Timeline

1. **Work window:** Friday – Thursday
2. **Snorkel processes:** Following week
3. **HireArt deposits:** ~1-2 weeks after billing week

### Example

```
Work window:       Fri Sep 19 – Thu Sep 25
Task accepted:     Wed Sep 24 ✓
Snorkel processes: Mon Sep 29
HireArt deposits:  ~Fri Oct 3
```

## Tracking Payment

### Status Meanings

| Platform Status | Meaning |
|-----------------|---------|
| Submitted | Task under review |
| Accepted | Task approved, queued for payment |
| Payout Submitted | Payment sent to HireArt |
| Paid | Complete |

### If Payment Delayed

If "Payout Submitted" shows for > 1 week and you haven't been paid:
1. Check your payment details are correct
2. Contact hr@hireart.com

## Important Notes

### Payment Basis

You are paid per **accepted submission**, not per submission.

An accepted submission must:
- Pass all automated checks (CI + LLMaJ)
- Pass peer review
- Meet difficulty threshold (< 80% pass rate)

### Rejection

Snorkel reserves the right to reject submissions based on the criteria in the project guidelines. You will have the opportunity to defend your submission if it's flagged for rejection.

### Disputes

Payment disputes should be directed to:
- **Project questions:** Slack `#ec-terminus-submission`
- **Payment questions:** hr@hireart.com
